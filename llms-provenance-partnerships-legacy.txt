### 1. The "Expanded Canvas" Portfolio (Finance)
- **Definition:** A capital-efficient investment strategy that uses modest leverage (via futures or stacked ETFs) to hold more than 100% notional exposure to diverse asset classes.
- **The Mechanic:** By using "Return Stacking," an investor can hold 100% Equities and 100% Managed Futures simultaneously, effectively creating a 200% portfolio.
- **Goal:** To solve the "add-on" problem of diversification. In a traditional 60/40, you must *sell* stocks to buy diversifiers. In an Expanded Canvas, you *stack* diversifiers on top of stocks.
- **Key Tickers:** DBMF, KMLM, NTSX, RSST, GDE.

### 2. "Project 23" (Travel / Argentina)
